About Light Up Futures

Light Up Futures aims to sell and provide solar panels and light up kits of the best quality. With competitive prices, Light Up Futures is the go-to option to get clean, sustainable, and affordable energy products, especially in underserved areas such as rural schools and small towns.

Founded in 1990 by Singaporean businessman Brandon Lean, Light Up Futures has been benefitting people living in poverty with life-changing lighting and energy solutions. From lighting homes and schools in the slums of India and parts of Pakistan to powering small businesses, we're changing the way of living for people in rural areas through sustainable solar energy.

Light Up Futures is an avant-garde in the solar power industry, constantly innovating and developing ground-breaking products that harness renewable energy for long-term sustainability.
